About us

ENERGY SOLAR ESTATE has been a participant in the Ukrainian renewable energy market since 2020 as an independent producer of electricity from renewable energy sources (RES). The company operates 1 solar power plant (ground-based) in the Dnipro region with a capacity of 3.69 MW, which was commissioned in 2019. The electricity produced by Energy Solar Estate LLC is purchased by the Guaranteed Buyer at a green tariff. At the end of 2020, the company's power plant produced 2.25 million kWh. "of green electricity.

Station information

The main technical characteristics of the generating equipment of SPP "ENERGY SOLAR ESTATE" are photovoltaic modules of JKM305M-60 type: unit power - 305 W; quantity - 12 096 pcs; - HUAWEI Sun2000-60KTLM0 inverters: unit capacity - 60 kW; quantity - 42 pcs; rated inverter capacity - 2,520 kW (limited by software to 2,500 kW). - The SPP has 3 complete transformer substations with transformers TM-1000/10/0.48kV;

Further electricity generation to the integrated power system is carried out through the networks of DTEK DNEPR ELECTRONIC NETWORKS JSC. The electricity produced is sold to the Ukrainian Wholesale Electricity Market at a green tariff.
